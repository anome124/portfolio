TOURISM

Tourism Board - PORTO

Heading back to my previous study, completed a mutual project with the tourism board of Porto, Portugal. Our project team was sent on a field trip to Portugal, where we conducted a two-week research, focused on the main highlights and tourism development of Porto.
After completely developing a marketing-plan report for the tourism board, we were requested to create a travel magazine, representing the main highlights, events and interesting facts of the city. The magazine was later officially published and used as a marketing campaign from the board. I took the initiative to put together the whole information and photo-material we collected during our trip in a magazine, created from scratch on Adobe InDesign.
To be honest, this was my first time using this platform, which was a challenge at first. However, it turned out to be a very interesting process that gave me the opportunity to learn new skills and gain precious and useful knowledge about Adobe Creative Cloud.
The project was a success and the Magazine was later used as an example for similar future projects in the university.
